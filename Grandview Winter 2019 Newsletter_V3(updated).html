THE VIEWFINDER:

Three Quick Snapshots from Grandview Cabinetry

A Brand New (Grand)View

As we usher in 2020, it’s a perfect time to think about vision. For the past 18 months, Grandview Cabinetry has been focused on building our vision for the future.


Everything’s new - our logo, our website, our product line-up, our finishes, our box construction methods, and even some of our internal systems.

 
 

An Eye for Texture and Style

With a sleek, contemporary look that rivals real wood; our Toronto door in textured laminate is durable and long-lasting. Not only is it resistant to chipping, scratching and dents, it’s also easy to clean and virtually maintenance-free.


Easy installation and a budget-friendly price point make this laminate door style a home-run for many multi-family properties.

Taking the Long View

Grandview got its start in 1946. In 2018, the Tidwell family reinvigorated the company as Grandview Cabinetry. As one of the largest employers in Parson, KS, we are committed to keeping jobs in the community and celebrating our many devoted, longterm employees.
Each year, we recognize those who have worked with us for 5, 10, 15, and 20+ years. In addition to plaques and gifts, our “20 Year Club” members are also invited to a special event in their honor. We’re committed to taking the long view with them - and with you.
